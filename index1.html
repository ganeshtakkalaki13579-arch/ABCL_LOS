Everything in one place

Built for every stakeholder in the lending chain

Relationship Managers, Business Analysts, Risk, the Credit Committee, Legal and Operations — each works the same case as it flows forward.

1

11‑stage guided pipeline

Each case advances one stage at a time — Sourcing, Case Login, BA/CAM, Risk, Credit Committee, Sanction, Customer Approval, CAD, Disbursement, PDD and NACH/ESCROW.

2

Smart committee routing

Cases are automatically routed through CC1 → CC4 based on ticket size and credit rating. Each committee must approve before the next reviews.

3

Automatic TAT tracking

Turn‑Around‑Time is captured at every stage and rolled up per case — so delays are visible at a glance on the pipeline board.

4

Shared reporting

Every account holder sees all cases, regardless of who sourced them, with filters by stage, status and sourcer.

5

One‑click Excel export

Download a full tracker — case details across all 11 stages plus stage‑wise TAT and a summary — as a genuine .xlsx, fully offline.

6

Stakeholder hand‑offs

Finished a stage? Notify the next team (BA → RA → CC …) by entering their email. Every message is logged in the Email Center.

7

Document uploads

Attach KYC, financials, the CAM, CAD and PDD documents right on the relevant stage, and download them back anytime.

8

Analysis & insights

Channel mix, disbursal‑vs‑login conversion, rejection reasons and zonal performance — all visualised on the Analysis tab.

About

A focused tool for Mid‑Market lending

This Loan Origination System digitises Aditya Birla Capital's credit workflow — incorporating the Credit Appraisal Memo (CAM) fields, committee structure and post‑disbursement steps — into a single, easy‑to‑use platform. Abbreviations are explained throughout, and a full glossary is one click away once you're in.
